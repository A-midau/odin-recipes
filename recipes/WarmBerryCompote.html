Warm Berry Compote

A delightful and easy dessert that's perfect for any occasion. This slow-cooked compote is bursting with the sweet and tangy flavors of fresh berries, enhanced by a hint of orange zest. Serve it warm over ice cream, yogurt, or pancakes for a truly indulgent treat.  

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups frozen mixed berries
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup orange juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons finely grated orange zest
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients: In a slow cooker, stir together frozen berries, sugar, orange juice, and orange zest.
  2. Cook: Cover and cook on High heat until the mixture starts to bubble, about 1 1/2 hours.
  3. Thicken: In a small cup, whisk together cornstarch and water until smooth. Stir this mixture into the berry mixture.
  4. Finish cooking: Cover and cook for another 5-10 minutes, or until the compote has thickened to your desired consistency.
  5. Serve: Enjoy warm or at room temperature.

Tip: For a richer flavor, consider using fresh berries if they're in season.
